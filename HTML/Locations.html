Locations





  • Locations allows you to link a Product to a specific target Location when creating a ticket.
  • From the Location main screen, click the button.




Create a new Location


  • On the Create Location screen, fill in your site information.
  • Once you've finished entering your site details, click the button to add your location and return to main menu.



Edit a Location


  • Highlight the field you would like to edit and click on the button to the right.



  • From the Edit Location screen, you will be able to modified any field. 
  • When finished editing, click or if you do not want to save the changes you made. 





Delete a Location


  • To Delete a Location, click on the button to the right for the Location you would like to remove.



  • You will be prompted to confirm the deletion. Reaffirm by clicking the button or if you do not want to delete the Location.
  • You will then be returned to the Location main screen and the Location has been removed.
