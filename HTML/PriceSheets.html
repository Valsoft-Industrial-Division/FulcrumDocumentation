Price Sheets






  • A Price sheet allows you to create one global price list that multiple Products can be set with different Rate Types, Start/End dates and Tax Codes. 
  • To create a new Price Sheet click the button at the top of the screen. 




Add Price Sheet



  • From the Create Price Sheet screen, fill in the Description and click on the button to add a new item.



  • A new line item will be added. Enter a Product and configure the other fields to fit your needs. 
  • If you would like to add another item to this Price Sheet, just click the button again.

1




Edit a Price Sheet



  • You will be able to edit the name of the Price Sheet, External ID or set the Price Sheet to Inactive.
  • You can add a new item or delete an existing item, but you cannot edit an existing item on the Price Sheet.




  • If you set a Min.Qty on a Price Sheet line item and there is a line item on your ticket below that minimum, the pricing calculations will use the Min.Qty by default.

Duplicate a Price Sheet



You can create a copy of an existing Price Sheet by clicking the button for the Price Sheet you would like to copy.





  • You will need to give the Price Sheet a New name. Unlike editing a new Price Sheet, all fields can be modified (including Start/End Date).
  • You can also add a minimum quantity to your price sheet. Once the minimum quantity is added, when paying a ticket you will see any lines that are effected by the minimum quantity you set.
  • If you set a minimum quantity and have an item for less than the minimum quantity, the pricing calculations will use the minimum quantity rather than the actual quantity.







Delete a Price Sheet



  • To delete a Price Sheet from the main Price Sheet screen, click thebutton for the Price Sheet you would like to remove. 





  • You cannot delete a Price Sheet or a detail line if it has already been applied to a Sales Order that has been applied to a ticket.





  • To delete a Price Sheet within the Edit Price Sheet screen, click the button for the Price Sheet you would like to remove. 
  • You will not see a confirmation screen, the item will just be removed.
  • You cannot delete a Tax Code if it has been used in a Price Sheet on a Sales Order that has been used on a ticket.
