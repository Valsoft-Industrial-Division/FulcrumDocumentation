Price Sheets







Price Sheets are used to setup product rates for ticketing and can be configured to set rates for as many Products as you would like. 

To create a new Price Sheet click the 'Create New' button at the top of the screen. 



 Each Product you add will (by default) show all rate types from the Rate Names menu.

 Entering a price for a rate is optional, but Product, UOM and Tax Code are mandatory fields

Product

Enter the Product you would like to add to the Price Sheet.

UOM

The unit of measure to convert your gross/tare weight to for ticketing.  
Name 
Name to identify your rate. By default, there are three rates already setup (Unit, Pay and Rate) that can be renamed to your preference.

Price 

Used to calculate the payment for the product at the time of ticketing. 

UOM

Allows you to chose how the rate is to be calculated (UOM, Quantity or Flat). 

Taxable

Toggle used to determine if the rate is taxable or not taxable. 

Min Qty.

 The minimum quantity for a product when ticketing. (i.e if your min qty is 23tn and a ticket is only 20tons - it is calculated at 23tons.








Add Price Sheet



Enter a Description for your Price Sheet and select an option to add the Product(s) you would like to have included in the Price Sheet.



Add a Single Products to a Price Sheet




To add a single Product click the "Add Product' button.


Select the Product, UOM and Tax Code and enter the rates/prices you would like.

When you're finished, click 'Save' or click 'Add Product' if you would like to add another product to your Price Sheet.




Add All Products to a Price Sheet



Click the 'Add all Products' button if you would like to add all active Products to the Price Sheet.



Set the UOM, Tax Code and enter any rates you would like on the Products.

When you're finished, click 'Save' or click 'Add Product' if you would like to add another product to your Price Sheet.




Add Products from a Product Class



To select all products from a product class, click the 'Add products from category' button.

*Note: This option is only applicable if you have Product Classes setup.



A window will open and allow you to select a Product Class. Enter the Product Class you would like to add the products from and click the 'Add Products' button.




All Products from your selected Product Class will be added to the Price Sheet.

When you're finished adding your rate info, click 'Save' or click 'Add Product' if you would like to add another product to your Price Sheet.





Edit a Price Sheet



You can edit any field on the Price Sheet, Add more Products, or delete a Product from a Price Sheet.






Duplicate a Price Sheet



You can create a copy of an existing Price Sheet by clicking the button for the Price Sheet you would like to copy.





 You will need to enter a new description for the duplicated Price Sheet (the name cannot be exactly the same as the Price Sheet you duplicated).

When you're finished configuring the Price Sheet, click 'Save' or click 'Add Product' if you would like to add another product to your Price Sheet.





Delete a Price Sheet



 To delete a Price Sheet from the main Price Sheet list, click the 'Delete' button for the Price Sheet you would like to remove. 




Click the 'Delete' button to remove the Price Sheet.




*Note: You cannot delete a Price Sheet if it has been used on a ticket.
