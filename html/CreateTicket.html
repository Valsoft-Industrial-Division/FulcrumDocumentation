Create Ticket





When creating a ticket, certain fields must be entered onto the ticket (Vehicle, Customer, Sales Order) in order to add ticket details. These are the essential components of the ticket which constitute a transaction. 

You can click on the button on select fields for quick lookup.


Number

Shows ticket number. If no number is entered, Fulcrum will automatically generate a ticket number for you.

Date

Shows current date.

Time

Shows current time.

Status

Select Ticket Receipt or Ticket Form. 

Site

Select the site the current ticket is for.

Staged Ticket

Enter a pre-configure ticket staging to allow for faster ticket creation.

Hauler 

Select a Hauler for your ticket.

Zone

Select a Zone for your ticket.

Vehicle

Select a Vehicle for your ticket.

Trailer

Select a Trailer for your ticket.

Customer

Select a Customer for your ticket.

Sales Order

Enter a valid Sales Order for the selected customer.

Remarks

Lets the customer input additional information.

Signature

Allows capture of a Signature on tickets.




Add Ticket Details



By default, you will see one ticket detail entry, click the Edit Details button to the right to enter product details.





From this window, you will select Product, Location, Inventory Container, UOM and Direction details as well as enter a manual Gross/Tare weight or Quantity.

If the vehicle has a stored tare weight, it is set automatically and you will not be able to edit it.

Click the at the bottom right of the screen to save your ticket details.





If you need to enter additional ticket detail, click on the icon to add another detail line. 




Click the  button to enter the ticket details




Enter your new ticket details and click on the at the bottom of the page.





The new ticket details have been added to the ticket.







Delete Ticket Details



Clicking the Delete icon will remove the ticket detail for the ticket.





Ticket Payment(s)



Once the ticket has all the details filled in, you can move to To see the Pay Details for a ticket, click at the bottom of the ticket screen.





The Payment screen will show all of the ticket details, a line total for each Product and a Ticket Total. 

You will also see any Escrow Balances (if applicable) and the Amount Due for this ticket.





There are four Payment options available; Cash, Check, Credit Card and Escrow.



Cash Payment



Select 'Cash' under Payment Type and enter the amount to be paid if the customer is making a cash payment.






Check Payment



Select 'Check' under Payment Type and enter a check number and the amount being paid if the customer is paying with a check.






Credit Card Payment



Select 'Credit Card' under Payment Type and enter the amount being paid. 

You can select a card already on file using the drop down arrow, or add a new Credit Card by clicking the 'Add new Credit Card' button.




Clicking the 'Add new Credit Card' button will bring up a new window for you to enter the credit card details. 

When finished, click the 'Create Vaulted Credit Card' button and you will be returned to the ticket screen and the new card will now be available for selection.





New Credit Cards will show in the Customers information details (under Main Menu-->Customers).







Escrow Payment


Select 'Escrow' under Payment Type and enter the amount being paid.

If the Customer has money in Escrow on their account, you will see the balance left in escrow.






Saving Tickets


If this is your first pass (i.e Inbound load), click to save the ticket and return to the main Ticket List. 

If you would like to create another ticket, click the button. 

Once the ticket has been saved and the customer has an email address in their Customer setup, a pdf copy of the ticket is automatically emailed to them.
